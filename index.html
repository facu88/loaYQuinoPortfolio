Hi!

We are ''Around The Songs'', a duo based in Cordoba, Argentina.
We are vocalist Loana Luz Plasencia and guitarist Facundo Felich, both professional musicians.
We have gigs with 250+ songs within 20 themed playlists combining vocal performances, Live instruments (2 Guitars,2 voices, different percussions) and Captivating acting skills through All Time Classics, Duets, Boleros, Salsa, Bossa Nova, Jazz, Blues, Top 40s, Latin, Pop, Rock, Ballads, Broadway and Movie Hits.

Loana Luz

I am Loana Luz Plasencia, professional singer, and musical educator from the city of Cordoba, Argentina. I'm 22 years old and my professional career as a singer started when I was 6 years old, I studied music specialized in choral singing for 12 years in college ''Instituto superior de educacion artistica Domingo Zipoli'' of Cordoba city.
Throughout development and artistic training at the school I was part of many concerts, recording disc, operas, musicals and I had a chance to sing in big naciolly and internacionally stages with musicians from all over the world. Some of these places was ''Otello''- opera 2013., ''Carmen''-opera 2014, Internacional Choirs Festival 2016'' (Santiago, Chile), ''Iguazu en concierto 2014'' (Foz, Iguazu), among many others. Currently I am working in 3 proyects: as singer of ''Around the songs'' Duo and with other proyect ''Amapola'' and my solist proyect. Also I'm teaching singing lessons and I'm working as vocal couching.

Facu Felich

My name is Facundo Felich, I am 32 years old. I studied guitar for 6 years at ‘’La Colmena’’ (a popular music school) of Córdoba City.
Once I finalized my courses, I started to play with diferent proyects and styles (Jazz, Bossa Nova, Pop, Rock, Tango) I am working as a session guitarrist for several artists in the city of Cordoba, both for recording and live performances. I’m currently a guitar teacher.
